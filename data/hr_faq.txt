Company HR FAQ

Annual Leave Policy:
- Full-time employees get 20 days paid annual leave per year
- Leave must be approved by manager 2 weeks in advance
- Unused leave expires at year end

Sick Leave:
- 10 days paid sick leave per year
- Medical certificate required for 3+ consecutive days

Benefits:
- Health insurance: 80% company paid
- Dental: Employee paid
- Gym membership reimbursement: up to $50/month
